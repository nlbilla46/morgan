Gaurav Parmar

I am a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University. I got my undergraduate degree in Computer Science (with highest honors) from University of California, San Diego in 2020.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Robotics. I am co-advised by Jun-Yan Zhu and Srinivasa Narasimhan . Prior to this, I have worked with Zhuowen Tu and Jack Silberman at UCSD.

I spent two wonderful summers (2021 and 2022) interning at Adobe Research with Krishna Kumar Singh, Yijun Li, Cynthia Lu, and Richard Zhang.
